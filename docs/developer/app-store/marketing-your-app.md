# Marketing Your App

**Source:** [https://developer.commerce7.com/docs/marketing-your-app](https://developer.commerce7.com/docs/marketing-your-app)

**Section:** app store

---
Once your app is built and approved, we want to help you get it in front of the right people. This page outlines how we can support your marketing efforts, how to use our brand assets, and what our approval process looks like.
* * *
## 
Brand Assets & Guidelines
Please adhere to the following rules when creating your marketing materials:
  * **Official Logo:** [Commerce7 Primary Logo](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1n2vgT-ljvWhyp3kI4YSQy7VjQaYBGxCg/view?usp=sharing)
  * **The Name:** Always refer to us as **Commerce7**. Please avoid using "Commerce 7" (with a space) or "C7".
  * **Visual Integrity:** Do not stretch, distort, or recolor the logo. Ensure there is ample clear space around the logo so it remains legible and prominent.


* * *
## 
Marketing FAQ
We receive many requests for co-marketing. To keep things fair for all partners, we have established the following standard procedures:
What we CAN do| What we CANNOT do  
---|---  
**App Marketplace:** We will list your app in our [App Marketplace](https://www.commerce7.com/partners#apps) for all customers to discover.| **Direct Email Blasts:** We cannot send dedicated emails to our customer base on behalf of a third-party app.  
**New Release Shoutout:** We often feature new apps in our monthly client newsletter.| **Dedicated Webinars:** We do not host or co-host dedicated webinars for individual integrations.  
**Featured Status:** We periodically feature new, top-performing or high-utility apps in high-traffic areas of the app store.| **Sales Outreach:** Our sales team does not perform direct outreach or "warm intros" for partner products.  
**Internal Enablement** We're happy to train our internal sales, marketing, and support teams on your app so they can recommend it to clients and prospects when appropriate. Please feel free to send assets, including PDFs and short (3-5 minute) videos for internal sharing.|

## 
Marketing Approvals
To ensure our brand is represented accurately, **all marketing materials mentioning Commerce7 must be approved** by our team before they are published. This includes:
  * Email campaigns & newsletters
  * Blog posts & Case studies
  * Press releases
  * Social media graphics


> **Submission Lead Time:** Please email your drafts to **[[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#b2c2d3c0c6dcd7c0f2d1dddfdfd7c0d1d7859cd1dddf)**. We request at least **2 business days** to review and provide feedback or approval.
