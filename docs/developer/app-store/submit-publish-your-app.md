# Submit Your App

**Source:** [https://developer.commerce7.com/docs/submit-publish-your-app](https://developer.commerce7.com/docs/submit-publish-your-app)

**Section:** app store

---
When your app is ready, the next step is to submit it for approval. After your version is configured and you've created your App Listing, you'll be able to submit your app.
# 
Requirements
Your app must following these requirements in order to be approved. If it does not, we will require revisions or reject your app.
  * ⭐️ Provides a service or tool that benefits more than one Commerce7 client
  * 🔒 App requests only the [API permissions](../app-platform/app-apis-webhooks.md) that are being used
  * 🐞 Has been tested and does not have bugs or any major problems
  * 🖌 If your app displays in Commerce7, that display must serve a purpose. App must usable in both light and dark mode (if applicable)
  * 📄 Follows Commerce7's [security standards](app-security-policy.md) and complies with the [Developer Terms & Conditions](https://www.commerce7.com/developer-terms).
  * ✍️ App Listing follows the [content guidelines](create-yourapp-listing.md)
  * 📱 Works responsively across multiple device sizes and is supported by modern browsers (if applicable)


In some cases, we may ask for changes that aren’t currently reflected in the requirements, based on your specific app. We want to ensure that all Commerce7 customers have a great experience using both our software and your app.
* * *
# 
Submitting your app
  1. From the app page, click **Submit App** from the top-right.
  2. Read through instructions and confirm
  3. Click **Submit App**


  * Your app will now be in a pending state and you will no longer be able to edit many of settings.


  4. Both the app and your listing will be sent to our team for approval.


  * Our team typically takes 3-4 business days to review and approve
  * If we find any issues with either the configuration or listing content, we'll email the developer who submitted the app to make revisions. If there are quite a few items that need updating, we will reject your app and have you test and review it further.


  5. Once your app has been approved, you'll receive an email notification and it will automatically be published on Commerce7.com and the App Store in Commerce7's admin.


* * *
# 
Approval process
We will review your app to determine if it follows our guidelines listed above. If we find any minor issues, you'll receive an email from us requesting changes. If we find any major issues and your app fails multiple guidelines, the approval process will stop and your app will be rejected until issues are rectified. In that case, we recommend you go through the list above.
Please note that since published apps are public for all wineries, Commerce7 will not approve an app that is built for only a specific client. While you can still use the App Development Center, you don't need to submit your app, and instead use your app with the client in ["Test" mode](../app-platform/test-your-app.md). Apps in test mode function the same but are not public.
