Need to give access to anyone to another developer? The Accounts area allows you to invite additional users to access and edit the app that you've created.
> 🚧
> ### 
> The user (email address) must already have a Commerce7 account. If they do not, first invite them to your sandbox account, or, if they're from a different company, have them request their own sandbox account.
## 
Invite to Commerce7
  1. In Commerce7 Admin, go to **Settings** > **Accounts**
  2. Click **Invite User**
  3. Enter their email address
  4. They'll receive an email to create a password with Commerce7


## 
Add a user to an app
  1. Once they have a Commerce7 account, click into an app
  2. From the left-hand menu, click **Accounts**
  3. The permission levels are **Admin Owner** and **Admin User**.  
Admin Owners have full access. Admin User can do everything except invite new users.
  4. Click **Send Invite**
  5. Enter an **Email Address** and click **Send Invite**
