# Installs

**Source:** [https://developer.commerce7.com/docs/installs](https://developer.commerce7.com/docs/installs)

**Section:** app platform

---
After the app is launched, you'll be able to view a list of clients that currently have your app installed in addition to the user who installed it and the price that they're paying in their local currency.
## 
Discounting the app price
You have the option to give a client a discounted price.
  1. On the **Installs** page, click on any client who has the app installed
  2. A modal will open where you can enter a **Discount Price**. This is the new amount that will be charged to the client and can be anything lower than the default app price. Enter "$0.00" to make the app free for the client.
  3. Click **Save Tenant**
  4. The new override price will display on the list page and this price adjustment will be used on all future invoices until it's removed.


**Removing a price adjustment**
  1. Click on the client from the **Installs** page
  2. Click the trash icon next to the discounted price.
  3. Click **Save Tenant**
  4. Future invoices will be charged the full amount.
