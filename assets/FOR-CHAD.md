# App Store listing files — ADC upload

Logo files in this folder:

- `logo-512.png` — use this one for the ADC App Store listing
- `logo-1024.png` — spare, same design at higher resolution
- `logo-512.jpg` — JPEG version if the ADC won't accept PNG

Screenshot files in `assets/app-store/`:

- `01-dashboard-overview.png` — upload as screenshot 1
- `02-insights-and-strategies.png` — upload as screenshot 2
- `03-integration-console.png` — upload as screenshot 3

## Where to upload in the ADC

When you're filling out the App Store listing section of the App Development Center, look for the **App Icon** or **Logo** upload field. Upload `logo-512.png`. Commerce7 typically wants a square image — this is 512×512 which is the standard size.

Then upload the three screenshots in the **Add at least 3 screenshots** section, in numbered order.

## Design

Blue square (`#0b7fab`) with a white "C" — matches the logo in the navbar on the marketing site exactly.

If Commerce7 ever asks for a different size or format, just reply back and we can regenerate quickly.
